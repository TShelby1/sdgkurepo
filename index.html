My name is Albert Lara and I am originally from Palm Springs California. Some people call me Albie. On my personal time I like to hangout with my girlfriend, try new food places, and explore with my dog. I currently work as a System Admin for Infinity laboratories. The company is a biotech company, and offers a numerous amount of services to pharmeceutical and medical companies. We also sterilize items that are intended to be in use during surgery. Although it is a medical company, I provide the IT expertise

I like where I work now but, personally I would prefer to be in a role where I know what is expected from me in that day. I DO NOT want to be in a role where there is no room for advancement. I consider myself a lifetime learner, so, I feel like this is a good field for me, with ever evolving technologies and practices.I hope I learn as much as I can from this course, and im excited to be here!!
